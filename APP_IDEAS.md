# Worldscope App Ideas

A living list of potential apps for the Worldscope platform. Apps range from simple data overlays to complex simulations and interactive experiences.

## Data & Monitoring
- **Earthquake Tracker** — Live USGS feed, magnitude filtering, historical playback *(in progress)*
- **Hurricane/Cyclone Tracker** — NOAA/JTWC feeds, storm paths, cone of uncertainty
- **Tsunami Warning System** — Real-time alerts, wave propagation simulation
- **Wildfire Monitor** — FIRMS/MODIS active fire data, burn scar mapping
- **Volcano Monitor** — Smithsonian GVP data, eruption alerts, ash plume tracking
- **Lightning Tracker** — Real-time lightning strike data (Blitzortung)
- **Ship Tracker (AIS)** — Live vessel positions, routes, port activity
- **Flight Tracker** — Live aircraft positions (ADS-B), flight routes
- **ISS Tracker** — Real-time International Space Station position and ground track
- **Satellite Tracker** — TLE data, orbit visualization for active satellites

## Satellite Imagery & Remote Sensing
- **NASA GIBS Imagery** — MODIS, VIIRS, true color, night lights, sea temp *(in progress)*
- **Sentinel Hub** — Copernicus Sentinel-2 imagery, NDVI, false color composites
- **Landsat Browser** — Historical Landsat imagery, change detection
- **SAR Imagery** — Sentinel-1 radar imagery for flood/ice mapping
- **Air Quality (Aerosol)** — MODIS/VIIRS aerosol optical depth

## Climate & Weather
- **Weather Overlay** — Temperature, wind, precipitation from GFS/ERA5
- **Climate Projections** — CMIP6 model outputs, warming scenarios
- **Sea Level Rise** — Coastal inundation under different rise scenarios
- **Ice Sheet Monitor** — Arctic/Antarctic ice extent, historical trends
- **Ocean Currents** — Animated particle flow from OSCAR/HYCOM data
- **Drought Monitor** — PDSI, soil moisture, vegetation health indices

## AI Model Inference (NVIDIA)
- **Earth-2 / FourCastNet** — AI weather forecasting via NIM
- **HelioFM Solar Viewer** — AI model of the sun, solar wind prediction
- **CorrDiff Super-Resolution** — Downscale coarse weather to high-res
- **Flood Simulation (Modulus)** — Physics-ML flood modeling
- **Fire Spread Prediction** — AI-driven wildfire spread forecasting

## Digital Twins & Simulation
- **City Digital Twin** — 3D building data + IoT sensor overlays
- **Traffic Simulation** — Vehicle flow modeling, congestion prediction
- **Power Grid Monitor** — Renewable energy production, grid load
- **Agricultural Monitor** — Crop health, irrigation, yield prediction
- **Supply Chain Viz** — Global logistics, shipping routes, bottlenecks

## Interactive & Game-like
- **Globe Risk** — Strategy game on the real globe (territory control, armies, alliances)
- **GeoGuessr-style** — Drop somewhere random, guess where you are using 3D tiles
- **Civilization Mode** — Place and grow cities, manage resources on real terrain
- **Disaster Response** — Timed scenarios: evacuate a coast before tsunami hits
- **Exploration Challenge** — Find hidden landmarks, earn badges for visiting places
- **Time Travel** — Scrub through historical imagery to see how places changed

## Education & Storytelling
- **Plate Tectonics** — Animated tectonic plate boundaries, earthquake/volcano correlation
- **Great Circles** — Interactive geodesic paths, flight route optimization
- **Climate Time Machine** — Visualize temperature/CO2/ice over geological time
- **Historical Borders** — Animated political boundaries through history
- **Space Missions** — Visualize launch sites, orbits, landing sites

## Utilities
- **Measurement Tool** — Distance, area, elevation profile between points
- **Coordinate Converter** — Click anywhere, get coords in multiple formats
- **Sun/Shadow Analysis** — Solar angle, shadow casting for any location/time
- **Viewshed Analysis** — What's visible from a given point and height
- **3D Model Placer** — Drop glTF models onto the globe at specific locations
